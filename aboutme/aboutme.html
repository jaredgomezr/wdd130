Biography

I'm a 30-year-old father and husband. I was born in California, and during my childhood, we moved to Guatemala, where I have lived ever since. I have a passion for learning, which has led me to work in various roles during the summers, including baker, shoemaker, mechanic, deliveryman, electrician, driver, and bricklayer. I also enjoy spending time with my family and gardening; they are my greatest motivation. Currently, I work for a Canadian company, where I am responsible for the training department, focusing exclusively on sales, covering Guatemala and San Salvador
